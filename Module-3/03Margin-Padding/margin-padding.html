Margin:-

        -> Margin is use to give space outside of element
        - margin: 5px; (top, right, bottom, left)
        - margin: 5px 15px; (top-bottom, left-right)
        - margin-top: 5px;
        - margin-right: 5px;
        - margin-bottom: 5px;
        - margin-left: 5px;

Padding:-

    -> Padding is use to give inside the element
        - padding: 5px; (top, right, bottom, left)
        - padding: 5px 15px; (top-bottom, left-right)
        - padding-top: 5px;
        - padding-right: 5px;
        - padding-bottom: 5px;
        - padding-left: 5px;
